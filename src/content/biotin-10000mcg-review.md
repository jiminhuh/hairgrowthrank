## How Biotin Actually Works for Hair Growth

Here's what the supplement companies won't tell you upfront: biotin only helps if you're deficient. It's not a magic hair growth potion.

Biotin supports keratin production, the protein that makes up your hair. If you're not getting enough biotin from food (eggs, nuts, seeds), supplementing can help. But if your levels are already normal, taking more won't suddenly give you Rapunzel hair.

I noticed subtle changes after about six weeks. My hair felt slightly thicker when I ran my fingers through it, and I found fewer strands in my brush. But we're talking incremental improvements, not dramatic transformation.

## Biotin vs. The Competition

### Biotin 10000mcg vs. Nutrafol

[Nutrafol](/go/nutrafol) is the luxury option in hair supplements. It contains biotin plus saw palmetto, marine collagen, and other ingredients targeting DHT (the hormone that causes hair loss).

Nutrafol costs about 20x more than biotin but addresses multiple causes of hair thinning. If you have pattern baldness or hormonal hair loss, Nutrafol makes more sense. If you just want to support general hair health on a budget, biotin wins.

I tested both for three months. Nutrafol gave me better results, but not $120-per-month better.

### Biotin 10000mcg vs. Rogaine Minoxidil 5%

[Rogaine Minoxidil 5%](/go/rogaine) is the gold standard for hair regrowth. It's FDA-approved and clinically proven to work for male and female pattern baldness.

Biotin and Rogaine work completely differently. Rogaine increases blood flow to hair follicles and extends the growth phase. Biotin supports the building blocks of hair.

You can use both together (I did), but Rogaine will give you more dramatic results if you have actual hair loss. Biotin is better for general hair health and thickness.

The downside? Rogaine requires twice-daily application forever. Stop using it, and any regrown hair falls out within months.

### Biotin 10000mcg vs. Vegamour GRO Serum

[Vegamour GRO Serum](/go/vegamour) is a plant-based topical treatment with ingredients like red clover and mung bean. It's positioned as a natural alternative to Rogaine.

At $58 per bottle, Vegamour costs more than biotin but less than Nutrafol. The application process is easier than Rogaine (once daily vs. twice), but the results are more subtle.

I found biotin gave me better value for money. Vegamour worked, but slowly, and the improvement wasn't worth the price difference.

### Biotin vs. Other Hair Serums

[The Ordinary Hair Serum](/go/ordinary) contains peptides and caffeine for $9. It's incredibly cheap but also incredibly weak. I used the entire bottle and saw zero difference.

[Rosemary oil](/go/rosemary) has some research backing its hair growth benefits. A 2015 study found it worked as well as 2% minoxidil. At $12 for a bottle that lasts months, it's worth trying alongside biotin.

## How to Use Biotin 10000mcg

Take one capsule daily with food. The timing doesn't matter much, but I found morning worked best since I could remember it with breakfast.

Don't take it with raw egg whites if you're into that for some reason. Raw egg whites contain avidin, which binds to biotin and prevents absorption.

If you're getting blood tests done, stop taking biotin at least 72 hours before. High biotin levels can interfere with certain lab results, especially thyroid tests.
